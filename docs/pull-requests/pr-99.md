Closing: not mergeable yet. TS 7 is the native compiler port, and our toolchain peers explicitly exclude it — `ts-jest@29` pins `typescript ">=4.3 <7"` and `typescript-eslint@8` pins `typescript ">=4.8.4 <6.1.0"`. So the Tests and lint jobs can't run against TS 7 regardless of the lockfile (which was also left out of sync here, breaking `npm ci`).

Adding a Renovate rule to hold the `typescript` major until `ts-jest` and `typescript-eslint` ship TS 7-compatible releases, at which point all three get bumped together in one PR.
